When the water was turned on inside the house, and the water valve was open at the hydrant, yet an attachment was not drawing water (for example, a connected hose with an end that was not open), a consistent leak occurred around the handle (some refer to it as leaking behind the handle). This meant that we couldn\u2019t connect a water sprinkle on a timer without wasting lots of water (as the hydrant was always on). <\/p>\n

We had two professional plumbers tell us that they\u2019d need to tear open the finished walls on the inside of the house and replace the whole unit to fix the problem. Not believing these claims, I went in search of a solution.<\/p>\n

One solution was to tighten the handle and was easy to perform. While that helped a bit, it wasn\u2019t the solution we needed.<\/p>\n
