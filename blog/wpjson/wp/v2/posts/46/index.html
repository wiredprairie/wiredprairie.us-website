<\/a> <\/p>\n

Hi to you USA Embassy! I’m not sure why the USA Embassy is in the USA, but hey, it’s so nice of them to write me!<\/p>\n

I’m glad I’m the owner of the e-mail address, otherwise the USA Embassy might be ticked-off that I’m reading their message.<\/p>\n

<\/a> <\/p>\n

My consignment box is in their custody? Well, how the heck did that happen? I better comply quickly so that the delivery will be effected immediately!<\/p>\n

FedEx Express is to deliver it? Great! I love love that company company. <\/p>\n

$195 to get my consignment box back and so they can effect the delivery to my home address! Sweet! What a bargain!<\/p>\n

<\/a> <\/p>\n

I guess I wasn’t fast enough — as the price has already increased to $295! That’s highway robbery — just to get my consignment box back! <\/p>\n

I see that there’s a text question and answer available. Feel free to use that if you need to today. <\/p>\n

But, this is the real annoying part — they forgot to include the MTCN Number I need for easy pick-up! <\/p>\n

<\/a> <\/p>\n
