Tips for presentations<\/a><\/p>\n

Of all of the tips — the one that consistently frustrates me is: FONT SIZE. “Can you see this?” Arrgh. JUST MAKE THE FONT BIG TO BEGIN WITH. Even though many in your audience CAN read your 9 pt font just fine, MOST would rather not if they had a choice. <\/p>\n

What have you seen recently that has driven you nuts at a presentation?<\/p>\n

I’ve seen far too many “readers” and too many that spend 2 or 3 minutes on walking through the agenda for a 1 hour presentation.<\/p>\n
