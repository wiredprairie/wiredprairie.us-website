Tonight (one day later on the 4th), we headed down to the basement to watch a recorded episode of AMCs Hell on Wheels and it was cold. Frak! What the\u2026<\/p>\n

The thermostat reported the temperature in the room was 62F. Seriously? It should have been about 66F at the time I looked.<\/p>\n

<\/a><\/p>\n

All of the evening settings (except Monday?) had disappeared completely. I had not made the change. I don\u2019t understand. I\u2019m no artificial intelligence expert (my wife though has a CS Master\u2019s degree with a specialty in it ), but I\u2019m confident any learning algorithm I would write wouldn\u2019t be this stupid and this broken.<\/p>\n
